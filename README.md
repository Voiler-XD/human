## 📋 What is human?

human lets you write instructions in plain English, then turns them into apps. It works on Windows computers. The apps it creates are production-ready, so you can use them for real tasks once built.

## 📖 How to Use human

Using human is simple:

1. Open the human app.

3. Click the "Build" button. human will process your instructions.
4. After a moment, human will create the app for you.

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

- **Human won’t start:** Restart your computer and try again. Make sure your system meets the requirements.
- **The build fails or creates errors:** Check your English instructions. Clear, short sentences work best.
- **Installer won’t run or shows errors:** Make sure you downloaded the full installer and have permission to install programs on your PC.
- **Links don’t open**: Try copying the link and pasting it into your browser.
